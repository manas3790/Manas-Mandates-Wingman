🔥 Core Rules

1. DM-Density Rule: The more DMs she gets, the less eager you must be.

2. Second-Glance Rule: If she looks twice, you approach in 30 seconds.

3. LOL Ratio Principle: 3 laughs = 1 number.

4. Yes Ladder: 3 yeses build rapport before the ask.

5. Exit Velocity Rule: Leave the convo on a high.

6. Non-Compliment Compliment: Compliment something unique she doesn’t expect.

7. Reverse Psychology: Suggest she offers the number.

🎓 Approaching Classmates

Skip the boring "What’s up?" Use lines like:

“If this prof had a fan club, you’d be president. You seem too focused.”

“You don’t look like you enjoy group projects either. We’d survive together.”

😏 Flirty Text Arsenal

“You’ve got dangerously good typing speed. Are you trying to seduce me with your grammar?”

“You’re the kind of chaos I’d cancel plans for. And I hate canceling plans.”

“You better stop texting like this… I might fall for your vocabulary.”

🕊️ Exit Without Damage

Be direct, not cruel: “You’ve been amazing, but I don’t see this going further. I respect you too much to pretend.”

Always leave her with dignity, not confusion.

🃏 Bonus: Flirty Games

1. Truth or Dare (Text Edition): Ask wild but clever questions.

2. 5 Rapid Fire: Play a challenge game like “Answer in 1 word or lose.”

3. 1 Lie, 2 Truths: Classic but spicy when done right.
